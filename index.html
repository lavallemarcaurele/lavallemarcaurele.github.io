Zombie survival

Face increasingly aggressive waves of zombies and survive solo or in cooperation with up to 8 players.

Improve your stuff

Upgrade your equipment and weapons to increase your chances of survival. With over 500 possible weapon customization combinations, nothing will stop you.

With the many maps each having their secrets, you are not ready to have done the trick, open your eyes.

Import your own textures to modify your soldier's skin, complete weapon missions to unlock weapon skins, and impose yourself on the field against other players.

Play on many different maps and try to kill as many zombies as possible, complete the waves and more, have fun!
